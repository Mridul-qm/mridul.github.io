Biography

I am presently doing my PhD in Physics from Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore. I did my Masters from IISc and Bachelors from KiroriMal College in Delhi University. My PhD research includes quantum computation which is a burning area of technology and science at the moment. I usually work with superconducting circuit architecture for the implementation of quantum computation. My work involves experimental realisation of superconducting circuits which could help in us in a certain problem for example implementation of a two qubit gate with a faster readout and short gate times.
